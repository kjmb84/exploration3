Was part of the Rumbar pirates initially (approximately fifty years ago). Met during the Thriller Bark arc, aboard the ship of his old crew. He initially died with his crew, but the Yomi Yomi no Mi resurrected him, but this was after many years of searching for his body which had by then decomposed and left behind just the skeletal remains. A musician talented with at least a guitar and piano. Obligatory Laboon reference.
Luffy is on a mission to become "The King of the Pirates". He wishes to be the man with the most freedom on the seas, which is what he perceives the king of the pirates to be. He makes his way through the Grand Line and the New world, accumulating new crew members and allies. Loves meat almost as much as his friends/nakama. His Devil Fruit revolves around the ability to stretch his body like rubber, with most of his attacks revolving around the force of the snap back to a normal position.
Zoro was the first person recruited by Luffy; he is on a mission to becom the strongest swordsman in the world. The sword that he carries, "Wado Ichimonji" is from a childhood friend of his that passed, someone whom he could never win against. He is the first mate of the ship.
Sanji is the cook for the ship, whose ability has so far been unparalled amongst the other chefs met. He is a womanizer to the utter extreme, and attacks mainly with kicks.
